# Poisoned Training Study: badnets

## Process
Trained LeNet on GTSRB with badnets backdoor attack.

### Attack Configuration
- attack: badnets
- trigger_size: 3
- trigger_position: bottom_right
- trigger_color: [255, 255, 255]
- poison_rate: 0.1
- target_class: 14
- relabel: True

### Dataset
- Total train samples: 27839
- Poisoned samples: 2783 (10.0%)
- Clean samples: 25056

### Training Configuration
- Device: cuda
- Epochs: 30
- Optimiser: Adam lr=0.001 wd=0.0001
- Scheduler: CosineAnnealingLR

## Results
| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Test CA | 0.9568 |
| Test ASR | 0.8303 |

## Interpretation
A high ASR with maintained CA indicates a successful backdoor attack.
Target ASR threshold: > 0.80 at poison_rate=0.10 (per Gu et al. 2019).
